Project overview

CardHub – smart invitation & template builder

CardHub is a full-stack mini project inspired by Canva and GreetingsIsland. It lets users browse categories of invitation templates, open a live preview with comments, then customise fonts, colours and backgrounds before downloading a share-ready invite image.

Developed by

Amit Singh & Naman Chausriya.

This project was created as an academic / portfolio-friendly web app to show practical knowledge of front-end design, back-end APIs and database integration.

Tech stack used

  • Frontend: HTML5, Tailwind CSS, modern UI components, smooth transitions.
  • Backend: Python Flask for routing, templates & sessions.
  • Database: SQLite with Flask-SQLAlchemy ORM.
  • Export: html2canvas to capture the card preview and download it as PNG.
  • UX extras: hover animations and touch / haptic feedback on taps (supported phones only).

What users can do

  • Browse templates by category (Birthday, Wedding, Engagement, Festivals, and more).
  • Open a template preview page with comments, likes and ratings.
  • Click “Edit this template” to go into the live editor.

Inside the editor

  • Change title, lines and the small corner label.
  • Choose font family, font size, bold / italic and text colours.
  • Pick a solid background colour or upload your own background image.
  • Download the card as a high-quality PNG image.

Social features

  • Users can register, log in and save customised cards to their profile.
  • Every template has its own comments / rating section on the preview page.
  • A community reviews page lists who commented on which card.
  • The Discover page shows trending, most-liked and most-commented cards.
